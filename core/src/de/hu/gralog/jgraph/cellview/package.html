This package defines {@link de.hu.gralog.jgraph.cellview.DefaultVertexRenderer} and {@link de.hu.gralog.jgraph.cellview.DefaultEdgeRenderer} that are used by GrALoG-Structures to render their vertices and edges. Renderers in GrALoG are {@link org.jgraph.graph.VertexRenderer} respectively {@link org.jgraph.graph.EdgeRenderer} that implement {@link de.hu.gralog.jgraph.cellview.DisplayModeRenderer}. Plugin-Developers who want to implement their own Vertex- or Edge-Renderers should first have a look at the JGraph-Documentation on how to write Renderers for JGraph.
